X Golf

Golf Year 'Round

X Golf America features some of the best technology for indoor golf simulators, making them the top choice for golfing year ‘round, and in any conditions. This video highlights the features that make X Golf the best place for golfers at any stage to practice their skills.

Baby Delight

Bring Mom Closer to Baby

Baby Delight is an industry leader in consumer goods for parents, and particularly mothers who are looking for safe and trustworthy products to use for their infants. This video highlights the client's "Beside Me Dreamer" Bassinets.

We wanted to sow connection between mother and child, and emphasize the importance of a quality and safe product. So, for this project, we went with a bright and warm look to compliment the feel of the Baby Delight brand and mission.

The Boys and Girls Club

Athletics Insider – National Flag Football Championship

The Boys and Girls Club is a widely recognized non-profit organization that helps to uplift and support the communities where they are located. They offer a variety of activities for families to participate in, including Flag Football Leagues.

This past year, the Boys and Girls Club was represented by the Northern RI Club at the NFL Flag Football Championship in Las Vegas, NV. This video recaps the values of the BGC of America in a short documentary style video surrounding the events of the Flag Football tournament.
